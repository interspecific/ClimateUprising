A Green and Just Federal Jobs Program

Rebuilding America Through Climate Work

This bold plan envisions a modern Civilian Climate Corps that creates dignified, living-wage jobs in ecological restoration, green infrastructure, and decarbonization—ensuring a just transition to a low-carbon economy.

Core Strategies

  • Establish a Civilian Climate Corps: Direct hires in urban forestry, habitat restoration, remediation, green infrastructure, and transit.
  • Guarantee fair wages and benefits: Living wages, healthcare, retirement, paid leave, and career pathways.
  • Prioritize equitable hiring: Focus on environmental justice communities, fossil fuel regions, Tribal nations, rural areas, and people facing employment barriers.
  • Empower local communities: Community-led project selection and oversight.
  • Integrate with workforce training: Partnerships with unions, schools, and apprenticeship programs.

Why It Matters

This program is not just about jobs—it’s about transforming the economy. It addresses climate, unemployment, and inequality together, creating durable opportunities for people and healing ecosystems nationwide.

Integrated Strategy

The jobs program complements restoration, remediation, transportation decarbonization, and environmental justice, creating a unified national approach to climate and economic renewal.
